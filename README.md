<h2><a class="anchor" id="overview"></a>Overview</h2>
This project analyzes digital transaction data from the PhonePe platform across financial services such as Loans, Insurance, Money Transfer, and Recharge & Bill Payments.

The objective is to convert raw financial data into actionable business insights using data analytics and visualization tools. The dashboard highlights key performance indicators such as transaction value, transaction volume, payment success rate, and failure reasons.

By identifying transaction trends, service performance, and operational issues, the project helps fintech businesses improve payment success rates, enhance user experience, and drive transaction growth.re used to formulate a data-driven strategy aimed at significantly improving both **rider and driver retention**.

---
<h2><a class="anchor" id="🛑 The Core Business Problem"></a>Business Problem</h2>

Digital payment platforms face challenges related to transaction failures, service performance variability, and operational inefficiencies:
- **Payment Failures:** Transaction failures due to insufficient balance, incorrect PIN, or server errors negatively impact user experience.
- **Revenue Loss:** Unsuccessful or failed payments lead to revenue leakage and reduced transaction volumes.
- **Service Performance Visibility:** Lack of clear insights into which financial services perform best makes it difficult to optimize fintech product strategies.
- **Customer Experience:** Frequent payment failures or delays may reduce customer trust and platform engagement.

<h2><a class="anchor" id="🔎Key Analytical Areas Addressed by the OLA Project"></a>Key Analytical Areas </h2>

High cancellation rates (both customer and driver-initiated) and inconsistent service quality directly impact OLA's revenue and market share. The analysis aims to address the following pain points::
- **💰 Transaction Volume Analysis:** Evaluation of total transaction count and transaction value to understand platform growth.
- **📊 Service Performance Analysis:**Comparison of transaction activity across Loans, Insurance, Money Transfer, and Recharge services.
- **❌ Payment Failure Analysis:** Identification of major causes of transaction failures such as server errors, wrong PIN, or insufficient balance.
- **📅 Time-Series Transaction Trends:** Analysis of monthly transaction patterns to identify growth trends.
- **🌍 Payment Success Monitoring:** Investigation of ride demand, cancellation spikes, and successful bookings across different geographic regions and time-based factors (e.g., peak Tracking successful vs failed transactions to evaluate platform efficiency.

<h2><a class="anchor" id="final-recommendations"></a>Final Recommendations</h2>

- **🚀 Improve Payment Reliability** Address major failure reasons such as server errors and incorrect PIN entries.
- **📈 Optimize High-Performing Services** Invest more resources in services generating the highest transaction volume.
- **💡 Enhance User Experience** Improve app notifications and payment processing speed to reduce transaction failures.
- **📊 Continuous Monitoring** Use data dashboards for real-time transaction monitoring and performance tracking.
